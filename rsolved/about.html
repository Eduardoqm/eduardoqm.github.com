What is this site?

     Making mistakes is common when writing code in R!
     Finding a solution on the internet is an investment in time that doesn't always work ...
     Therefore, this site is a search engine for programmers, academics and those who use R!
     
     Here you go straight to the point! You don't have to mine information on Google!
     
     All search results here are exclusively about the R language!
     So when you search for a certain function, package or error, it won't show up
     single results from other languages ​​or from other themes that are not linked to the R language.      
     

How to use it?

     Just type in the search bar what you are looking for.
     You can check for errors by copying directly from your R console,
     You can look for how to make a certain type of graph,
     How to use a certain function, among other infinite possibilities!
